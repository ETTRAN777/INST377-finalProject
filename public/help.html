Job Junction

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Job Junction?

Job Junction is a platform for discovering, searching, and bookmarking IT jobs. It connects job seekers with employers and helps you build your career with ease.

How do I search for jobs?

Use the search bar and filters on the main page to find jobs by position, location, date posted, and salary range.

Be sure to select the Search Bar and click Enter for the filters to take effect (even if your search bar is empty)

Click on a job card to see more details and apply.

How do I bookmark jobs?

Click the bookmark icon on any job card to save it. You can view all your bookmarked jobs on the Bookmarks page.

Do I need an account?

You can browse and search jobs without an account, but creating an account allows you to save bookmarks across devices.

Who can I contact for support?

Click the help icon in the corner or visit the About page for more information and support options.
